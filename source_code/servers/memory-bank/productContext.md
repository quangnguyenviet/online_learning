# Product Context

## Problem Statement
Traditional education often lacks the flexibility and accessibility required by modern learners. Instructors also face challenges in finding reliable platforms to host their content, manage students, and monetize their expertise without high overhead.

## Solution: Vitube
Vitube addresses these issues by providing a centralized, scalable platform for digital education. It simplifies the end-to-end process from content creation to consumption.

### For Instructors
- **Ease of Use**: Streamlined course upload and organization.
- **Analytics**: Insights into course performance and student engagement.
- **Monetization**: Clear tracking of payments and registrations.

### For Students
- **Structured Learning**: Courses broken down into lessons with clear objectives.
- **Flexibility**: Access to high-quality video content anytime, anywhere.
- **Achievement**: Trackable progress to stay motivated.

## User Personas
1. **Students**: Individuals looking to acquire new skills through structured video courses.
2. **Instructors**: Content creators and educators who want to reach a wider audience.
- **Administrators**: System operators who manage users, categories, and overall platform health.
